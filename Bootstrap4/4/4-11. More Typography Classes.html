  • .text-*-left :

    Responsive Left Aligned Text

    Note that text is left-aligned by default.

    .text-left : Left aligned text on all screen sizes.

    .text-sm-left : Left aligned text on small or wider screens.

    .text-md-left : Left aligned text on medium or wider screens.

    .text-lg-left : Left aligned text on large or wider screens.

    .text-xl-left : Left aligned text on xlarge or wider screens.
